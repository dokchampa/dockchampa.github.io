"métadonnées"


School Project 2023

For this project, I had to imagine a trend book around the theme of metadata. To do this, I decided to explore metadata through four different approaches:

Visual: How metadata is represented in the imagination through AI. Narrative: How metadata can tell a story about an image using its information.

Experimental: Metadata as a graphic medium and how to use it in different aspects.

Interactive: Engaging the user with metadata by "repairing" the photo and retrieving the lost information from the photo.
